HP-Based Damage — 6 étapes (Yuko v1.2)

① Raw = TargetHP × HP%/100
② Adjusted = Raw × PvPFactor (si PvP)
③ ATKRef = (ATK−DEF) × BasicMult%/100 (sans RES ni crit)
④ Min = ATKRef×0.8, Max = ATKRef×50 (HP courant) ou ×100 (HP max)
⑤ Clamped = clamp(Adjusted, Min, Max)
⑥ Final = Clamped ÷ PvPFactor (pas de RES, pas de crit)
