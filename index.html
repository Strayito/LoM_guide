La Formule de Dégâts The Damage Formula Vzorec poškodenia
STEP 1 — Calculate ATK & DEF ATK = (FinalATK × (100 + Parking%) / 100) × (100 + Buff%) / 100 + Exclusive STEP 2 — Base Damage A = (ATK − DEF) × FinalMult × DMG% / 100 STEP 3 — Critical B(Crit) = A × CritMult / CritRES STEP 4 — Resistance C = (A or B) × (100−RES%) / 100 × (100−DMGRES%) / 100 STEP 5 — Final Output PvE = C × Bonus (Sword 1.1 / Ax 1.05)
