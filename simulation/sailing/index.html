Formule Sailing / Canon (Yuko v1.2 p.13-14)

FinalMult = CaptBasicATKDMG%/100 × (1+CaptDMGRES%) × (1+Ship%+Hull%)
Basic = (CaptATK−EnemyDEF) × FinalMult × (1−CannonRES cap60) × (1−CaptDMGRES cap80)
Combo = Basic × CannonCombo%/100 × (1−CaptComboDMGRES%)
CaptBasicATKDMGRES augmente les dégâts (mécanique inverse, confirmé PDF).
