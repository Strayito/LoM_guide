Référence des FormulesFormula ReferenceReferencia vzorcov

STEP 1ATK/DEF = (FinalBase × (100 + Parking%) ÷ 100) × (100 + Buff%) ÷ 100 + Exclusive
STEP 2FinalMult = (Skill% + BuffAdd%) ÷ 100 × (100 + BuffMult%) ÷ 100 + BuffExcl% ÷ 100
STEP 3A = (ATK − DEF) × FinalMult × DMGMult% ÷ 100
STEP 4B(Crit) = A × CritMult ÷ CritRES  |  B(SkillCrit) = {A × (1 + SkillCritMult)}0.98
STEP 5C = (A or B) × (100−RES%) ÷ 100 × (100−DMGRES%) ÷ 100 × (100+BuffFinal%) ÷ 100
STEP 6PvP = C ÷ PvPDecrease  |  PvE = C × PvEBonus (Sword 1.1 / Ax 1.05)  |  ToBoss = PvE × (100+Boss%) ÷ 100
